About Machion Jackson

At the helm of Detroit Public Schools' operations, my focus lies in steering strategic planning, direction, and supervision across departments critical to our educational mission.

My Philosophy & Impact

The responsibility for managing a general operating budget of $170 million and capital budgets totaling $823 million reflects my expertise in operation efficiency, budget management and fiscal oversight. With expertise that spans over two decades in leadership roles, my approach to fostering collaborative problem solving and process enhancement has been pivotal.

YIE's efforts in community engagement and consensus building have empowered stakeholders, driving districts' visions for high-performing educational environments. YIE's approach blends strategic foresight with thoughtful execution, ensuring projects not only meet but exceed expectations.
